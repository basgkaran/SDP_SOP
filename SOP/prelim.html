Preliminary Configuration

Checklist to prepare your environment and stakeholders before implementation

Environment & Access

Tenant Setup: Ensure your SDP Cloud environment is provisioned with admin login.
Credential Readiness: Collect admin and integration account credentials securely.

Stakeholder Alignment

SPOCs: Appoint single points of contact from IT, HR, Facilities, and Security teams.
Kickoff Call: Schedule and conduct to finalize timelines, owners, and responsibilities.

Legacy System Audit

Source Data: Export requester and ticket data from Jira/ServiceNow/Email systems.
Data Format: Ensure fields align with SDP import format. Clean up duplicates and nulls.

Data Preparation

Requester Sheet: Format employee details (Name, Email, Department, Site).
Technician Sheet: Map roles and assign to modules/groups accordingly.

Core Configuration Inputs

Sites & Departments: Create per-location structure in advance for SLAs and approvals.
Business Hours: Set up working calendars and holidays to define SLA windows.
Issue/Service Categories: Gather functional classifications with parent-child format.

Discovery & Asset Prep

Asset Import: Collect device inventory (via Endpoint Central, AD, or CSV).
Asset Classification: Define categories like Laptop, Server, Switch, License, etc.

Risks to Avoid

Late Data Submission: Can delay system provisioning and test runs.
Unclear SPOCs: Results in miscommunication during build and go-live support.
Category Confusion: Broad categories lead to misrouted tickets — be specific!
