Overview

The Knowledge Base (Solutions) module provides a centralized repository for documented solutions, how-tos, and troubleshooting guides, improving self-service and reducing repetitive tickets.

Navigation – Full Paths

Solutions: Solutions → All Articles

Add Solution: Solutions → Add New

Configure Categories: Admin → Knowledge Base → Categories

Approval Workflow: Admin → Knowledge Base → Approval Rules

Feedback & Ratings: Admin → Knowledge Base → Settings

Key Configurations

Solution Categories: Group articles by department or topic (e.g., IT Support, HR, Security).

Approval Workflow: Enable pre-publish review and approval to maintain quality and accuracy.

Article Format: Use templates that include symptoms, cause, resolution, impact, and screenshots.

Permissions: Control who can create, approve, and access public/private articles.

Feedback Settings: Enable thumbs-up/down and comment section for user feedback.

Best Practices

Use searchable keywords: Tag articles with synonyms, acronyms, and issue codes for better search results.

Keep content concise: Use bullet points, short paragraphs, and headings for readability.

Review articles periodically: Update aging or outdated content every 6 months.

Encourage feedback: Use ratings/comments to identify valuable or poor-performing articles.

Risks & Mitigation

Unapproved content: May spread misinformation. Always enable approval before publishing.

Duplicated solutions: Reduces effectiveness. Use tags and categories properly and assign reviewers.

Low usage: Track article views and feedback to prune or promote content as needed.

End User View

End users can browse, search, rate, and comment on solutions via the self-service portal. Helps resolve issues faster without logging tickets.

Technician/Admin View

Technicians can contribute articles, link them to tickets, and share them with users. Admins manage category structure, approvals, and performance reports.
