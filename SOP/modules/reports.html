Overview

Reports and dashboards in ServiceDesk Plus enable tracking KPIs, SLA adherence, technician performance, ticket trends, asset insights, and overall ITSM health. Use both built-in and custom reports for decision-making.

Navigation – Full Paths

Reports: Reports → All Reports / New Report

Dashboards: Home → Dashboard

Custom Reports: Reports → New Report → Choose Module

Scheduled Reports: Reports → Schedule Report

Zoho Analytics: Admin → Integrations → Advanced Analytics

Key Configurations

Default Reports: Use built-in reports for incidents, SLAs, surveys, asset lifecycle, etc.

Custom Reports: Create detailed reports with filters, grouping, charts (bar, pie, stacked).

Scheduling: Automate report delivery via email to key stakeholders (daily, weekly, monthly).

Export Options: PDF, XLSX, CSV for compliance and analysis.

Dashboards: Interactive widgets show open tickets, SLAs, overdue items, and trends across services, sites, or technicians.

Best Practices

Define KPIs: Set SLA adherence, resolution time, first-call resolution, backlog ratio, etc., as measurable metrics.

Use color-coded widgets: Red/yellow/green status tiles give instant visibility.

Group reports: Group reports by audience (e.g., Management, L1 Team, Asset Team) for clarity.

Leverage Zoho Analytics: For advanced joins, cross-module KPIs, or external data blending.

Risks & Mitigation

Incorrect filters: May cause misleading KPIs. Validate reports with raw data during setup.

Unscheduled reports: Leads to manual follow-ups. Schedule delivery to automate reviews.

Excessive dashboard clutter: Use only relevant KPIs per role to avoid visual overload.

Technician/Admin View

Technicians see operational dashboards and performance KPIs. Admins manage global dashboards, configure reports, and integrate with Zoho Analytics for deep analytics.
