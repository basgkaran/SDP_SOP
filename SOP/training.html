Training Plan - Stakeholder Enablement

Prepare users across roles to adopt and operate ServiceDesk Plus effectively

Training Objectives

Ensure each user group understands their workflows, interfaces, responsibilities, and tools available in ServiceDesk Plus. Reduce resistance, boost adoption, and minimize early support load.

Admin Training

Audience: IT Admins, Platform Owners

Focus: Full platform configuration, templates, SLA setup, automation, roles, technician management, CMDB, and asset tracking.

Tools: Admin Manual, System Design Document, Train-the-Trainer slides

Duration: 2–3 hours

Technician Training

Audience: Service Desk Agents, Facilities/IT Tech Teams

Focus: Logging and updating tickets, using dashboards, SLAs, queues, approvals, worklogs, solution articles, tasks, and change/problem management.

Tools: Technician Manual, Use Case Videos, Live Demo

Duration: 1.5 hours

Requester (End-User) Training

Audience: All employees using the portal

Focus: Accessing the portal, raising tickets, using the service catalog, searching knowledge base, tracking requests, giving feedback.

Tools: Quick Start Video, FAQ PDF, Self-Service Portal Demo

Duration: 30–45 minutes

Delivery Plan

Method: Live sessions (in-person or Zoom), followed by recordings

Track Attendance: Use sign-in sheets or LMS if available

Follow-up: Refresher sessions at 2-week and 1-month intervals

Best Practices

Use role-based demos: Avoid overwhelming users with features not relevant to their role

Record sessions: For future onboarding and just-in-time training

Create a knowledge base for training: Upload all materials in the KB and link from the portal
