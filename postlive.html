Post Go-Live Support & Optimization

Ensure platform stability, adoption, and continual service improvement

Initial Monitoring & Hypercare

Monitor SLAs: Check if ticket resolutions meet SLA targets. Tune automation if needed.

Track Ticket Volumes: Compare pre/post go-live data to assess usage and backlog.

Analyze Feedback: Use technician and requester feedback to find friction points.

Live Dashboard Monitoring: Keep dashboards active for critical metrics like overdue, unassigned, breached SLAs.

Issue Resolution & Enhancements

Log Post-Go-Live Issues: Maintain a tracker for config issues, bugs, missing fields, or permissions.

Prioritize Changes: Only address high-impact issues in production; defer enhancements to phase 2.

Weekly Review: Assess pending fixes and validate resolution turnaround.

Training & Communication

Refresher Sessions: Conduct short training sessions for new users or teams who missed initial training.

FAQs: Create a living FAQ in the knowledge base and keep adding to it.

Communicate Enhancements: Share platform updates and new features clearly with users.

Continuous Improvement

Run Monthly Governance: Hold governance meetings to review metrics, feedback, and decide enhancements.

Review Workflows Quarterly: Review RLCs, approvals, business rules every quarter to keep them aligned with business processes.

Adoption Dashboard: Track who uses the portal, what templates are used most/least, and identify gaps.
