Overview

This module helps IT teams manage contracts and purchases by offering central tracking, renewal alerts, association with assets, and PO lifecycle management.

Navigation – Full Paths

Contracts: Assets → Contracts

Purchase Orders: Assets → Purchase → All POs

Vendor Management: Admin → Purchase & Contracts → Vendors

Alert Setup: Admin → Asset Management → Contract Notification Rules

PO Approval Rules: Admin → Purchase & Contracts → PO Approval Workflow

Key Configurations

Contract Types: Support, Warranty, Lease, Subscription. Helps filter and report.

Contract Fields: Track terms, renewal date, value, vendor contact, alerts, and associated assets.

PO Workflow: Multi-stage approvals, line items, vendor details, and status tracking (Draft → Issued → Received → Closed).

Vendor Profiles: Maintain contact info, contracts list, performance notes, and support SLAs.

Best Practices

Use alerts 30/60/90 days in advance: Avoid surprise expiries by proactively tracking contract dates.

Link contracts to assets: Track coverage and dependencies for devices and services.

Classify vendors: Use tags like “Tier 1 Vendor”, “Recurring”, etc. for better governance.

Standardize PO templates: Ensure all POs follow a structured format with key sections like payment terms, delivery SLAs.

Risks & Mitigation

Missed renewals: Enable and test alert configurations. Use backup emails if primary recipient is on leave.

PO status mismatch: Enforce updates when materials are received to close open POs.

Unlinked contracts: May impact audit and traceability. Always link assets, POs, and vendors.

Technician/Admin View

Technicians track POs and update delivery status. Admins manage contract renewals, vendor compliance, and receive alerts for actionable renewals.
